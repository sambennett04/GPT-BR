Title: The Family Finance application crashes if the View is changed in the Report Section.
      
Observed Behavior: On the Display of Incomes by Articles pop-up of the reports screen, if the user changes the appearance of the report from "view values" to use percent values", the application crashes.

Expected Behavior: The appearance of the report should be changed to "use percent values".

Steps To Reproduce:
    1. Open app
    2. Click allow button
    3. Click Report
    4. Click Incomes by Articles or Expenses by Articles
    5. Click View Button
    6. Click view values check box
    7. Click use percent values checkbox
    8. Click OK

Additional Information:
Device: Oppo Neo 7
OS: Android 6.1
